Our Causes

Areas of Impact

Education

With this objective, Pure Hearts set up an integrated project for education enhancement in Hamara Gurukuls run by Pure Hearts for Children in Bastis as mobile learning centers at Sector 62, Sector 51 and Sector 50 (3 centers) in Gurugram. Our goal is to integrate these children into mainstream educational institutions. Once integrated, we support them in their homework and nurture their zeal to study by holding evening classes.

Women's Health

When women switch to menstrual cups, it not only impacts their hygiene but also reduces non- biodegradable sanitary waste from entering our landfills. Gurujal & District Administration of Gurugram have recognised Pure Hearts for their efforts towards a sustainable environment and supporting the weaker section of the society.

Environment

We run several drives for tree plantations each year. Pure Hearts campaigns address environmental issues and support greenery-cleanliness-health. These include awareness drives on waste management, go-green, pollution, plastic, sustainability, saving rivers, water conservation-rainwater harvesting, farmers workshops, land restoration initiatives.

Healthcare

Pure Hearts set up a system to support the government machinery by doing doorstep RTPCR tests. If these came positive, then with the support of volunteer doctors, would support With FREE online consultations, ration kits, and medicines. Our volunteer nurses would visit the patients in the Bastis and show them how to use the oxygen monitors and help them understand what medicines to take, as prescribed by the doctors.
